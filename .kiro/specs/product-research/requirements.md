# Product Research: Awareness-First Growth Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Goal**: Transform SKIDS Parent from a PHR tool to a **parent education platform** that builds awareness first, then converts to paid services.

**Strategy**: Open the free version wide, create daily educational content, and use it as a marketing funnel to build trust and drive conversions.

---

## Current State

### What Works
- PHR (Milestones, Growth, Habits, Vaccinations)
- Dr. SKIDS AI Chatbot
- 16 organ discovery modules
- H.A.B.I.T.S. framework
- Blog integration (external API)
- Lead capture → Neodove CRM

### What's Missing for Awareness Growth
- **No user onboarding flow** - Parents jump straight into PHR
- **No "Health Score" metric** - No clear value metric
- **No community features** - Parents can't share milestones
- **No referral program** - Missed viral growth
- **No push notifications** - Users miss reminders
- **No PDF export** - Can't share health records

---

## Awareness-First Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Open the Free Version (Weeks 1-2)

#### 1.1 Remove All Paywalls on Free Content
- **Daily blog posts** - Fully accessible without login
- **Organ discovery modules** - Fully accessible without login
- **H.A.B.I.T.S. framework** - Fully accessible without login
- **Milestone tracker** - Free for all children (no limit)
- **Growth tracker** - Free for all children (no limit)
- **Habits tracker** - Free for all children (no limit)

#### 1.2 Add "Health Score" Metric
- Overall health score (0-100)
- Breakdown by category (nutrition, growth, development)
- Monthly progress charts
- **Free for all users**

#### 1.3 Add PDF Export
- Downloadable comprehensive reports
- Shareable with pediatricians
- Export to WhatsApp/email
- **Free for all users**

### Phase 2: Build Awareness (Weeks 3-6)

#### 2.1 Daily Educational Content
- **Daily blog post** - 1 new post per day
- **Organ of the Day** - Rotate through 16 organs
- **Habit of the Day** - Rotate through 6 habits
- **Milestone of the Day** - Age-specific milestones
- **Video tips** - 60-second health tips

#### 2.2 Social Sharing
- **Share milestones to WhatsApp** - One-click sharing
- **Share growth charts** - One-click sharing
- **Share habit streaks** - One-click sharing
- **Share blog posts** - One-click sharing
- **Referral program** - Reward for sharing

#### 2.3 Community Features
- **Parent community** - Group discussions by age group
- **Milestone sharing** - Private/family-only sharing
- **Expert Q&A** - Weekly live sessions

### Phase 3: Convert to Paid (Weeks 7-12)

#### 3.1 Tiered Pricing Model
- **Free Tier**: All PHR, daily blog, organ discovery, H.A.B.I.T.S.
- **Premium Tier** (₹299/month): AI Chatbot, teleconsults, PDF export, push notifications
- **Family Tier** (₹499/month): 3 children, priority support, doctor network

#### 3.2 Conversion Triggers
- **PHR completion** - "Complete your PHR to get full insights"
- **Habit streak** - "Keep your streak going with Premium"
- **Milestone tracking** - "Get personalized milestone alerts with Premium"
- **Growth trends** - "See growth trends with Premium"

#### 3.3 Free Trial
- **7-day free trial** for Premium
- **No credit card required**
- **Auto-cancel anytime**
